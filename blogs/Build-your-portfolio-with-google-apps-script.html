Build your portfolio with google apps script

Are you looking for a way to streamline your workflow and save time on repetitive tasks? Look no further than Google Apps Script, a powerful scripting language that allows you to automate processes and create custom applications within Google's suite of productivity tools. With Google Apps Script, you can build a wide range of custom automation to supercharge your productivity and take your work to the next level.


How Google Apps Script can Supercharge Your Productivity with Custom Automation
One of the biggest advantages of using Google Apps Script is the ability to automate repetitive tasks. For example, if you find yourself repeatedly copying and pasting data between Google Sheets, you can use Apps Script to create a custom function that does the work for you with just a single click. This can save you hours each week, freeing you up to focus on more important tasks.


But the power of Google Apps Script goes far beyond simple task automation. With this scripting language, you can create custom applications that integrate seamlessly with other Google services, such as Google Docs, Google Drive, and Google Calendar. For example, you could use Apps Script to build a custom workflow that automatically creates a new document in Google Docs whenever you receive an email with a certain keyword in the subject line.
